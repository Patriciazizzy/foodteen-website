APPLICATION

This application provides a more easier and smooth transaction ordering food in a school canteen. This will help users to avoid crowd and saves time purchasing their product.

You will be able to order food in this application by choosing the product you want to purchase.
In this application you just need to register and log in to the app so you can browse to the menu
and add to cart your chosen food and click to order.
