Menudo


This is a recipe for basic pork menudo. It is a delicious dish that makes use pork, liver, and vegetables such as carrot and potato.

Ginataang Gulay


Ginataang gulay is commonly made-up of kalabasa (Calabaza squash) and snake beans. Other seasonings and ingredients are also added to complete the dish and make it taste good.

Chicken Adobo


Chicken Adobo is a type of Filipino chicken stew. Chicken pieces are marinated in soy sauce and spices, pan-fried, and stewed until tender. The dish gained popularity because of its delicious taste and ease in preparation.

Bicol Express


Bicol Express is pure comfort food! With pork cubes cooked in coconut milk and chili peppers, it's rich, creamy, spicy and delicious. Serve with steamed rice for a hearty and big flavored meal!

Sitaw with tokwa


Adobong Sitaw with Tofu is another version of Adobong Sitaw, this time it is cooked with fried extra firm tofu. Adobong Sitaw is a Filipino vegetable dish

Fried Egg


A fried egg is a cooked dish made from one or more eggs which are removed from their shells and placed into a frying pan and fried. They are traditionally eaten for breakfast in many countries but may also be served at other times of the day.

Mixed veggies with quail eggs


Sipo Egg with mixed vegetables, quail eggs smothered in a rich, creamy sauce is the ultimate vegetable dish. It's easy to make, budget-friendly, and tasty. Delicious on its own or with steamed rice!

Eggplant Omelet


Tortang Talong is a simple yet delicious dish that has become a favorite among Filipinos. Its versatility and affordability make it a popular choice for families looking for a quick and satisfying meal.

Lumpiang Shanghai


Lumpia or Lumpiang Shanghai is a type of Filipino egg roll and the favorite of taong bayan. The basic filling is composed of ground pork along with minced onions, carrots, and seasonings such as salt and ground black pepper.

Hotdog


The quintessential summer food, hot dog is an appetizer that can easily replace a hearty meal. It makes a tasty addition to main dishes and easily turns into fun snacks like Crunchy Cheese Dog Bread Roll snacks the whole family will love.

Longsilog


Longsilog is a combination of longganisa sausage, sinangag (garlic rice), and itlog (fried eggs). The Filipino-style sausages (pork, garlic, vinegar, salt, sugar) are boiled and fried in their own fat until crispy, while the garlic rice and the eggs are fried in oil.
